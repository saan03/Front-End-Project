Welcome to the Open Source Club

Discover Innovation at BSA Crescent Institute of Science & Technology

Embark on an exciting journey with the Open Source Club of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at B S Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science & Technology. Our club is a gateway to exploring the limitless possibilities of technology through open-source projects and collaborative learning.

Innovate Together

At the Open Source Club, we are dedicated to fostering an environment where passion for technology meets proficiency in open-source development. We believe in the power of community and the boundless opportunities that arise when creativity and coding converge.

We Offer

Technical Workshops: Participate in hands-on workshops and dive deep into various technologies, coding languages and gain practical skills that set you apart.

Bootcamps: Join our intensive bootcamps designed to fast-track your learning. If you're looking to enhance your expertise, our bootcamps offer immersive experiences in coding and development

Coding Challenges: Test your skills and push your limits with our exciting coding challenges. Compete in problem-solving contests and showcase your abilities while earning recognition and rewards.
